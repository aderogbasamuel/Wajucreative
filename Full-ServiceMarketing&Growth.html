What's Included

Let's break it down path by path - with a clear explanation of how it all works together to grow your brand

1

Social Media Marketing

Your brand deserves more than random posts

This is the frontline of your business. It's where people meet you, connect with your message and decide whether to follow, engage, or click through. But to make that happen, your presence must be intentional, aligned, and consistent.

Here's what I handle:

  • Platform selection (IG, TikTok, FB, LinkedIn, etc.) based on your industry and audience
  • Monthly content strategy built around your offers, events, promos, and goals
  • Branded visuals: flyers, carousels, quotes, reels all aligned with your voice
  • Strategic captions, hashtags, and call-to-actions (CTAs)
  • Posting schedule (automated or assisted)
  • Weekly or bi-weekly performance check-ins

How it works with everything else:

Social media becomes the entry point into your brand. It's where people get curious and start paying attention. It links directly to your website, email opt-in, and sales funnels - making it the hub of your marketing.

2

Content Creation

Content that not only looks good - it builds trust and drives action

This is where your voice meets visuals. Content is not just what you post on social media - it includes launch graphics, ads, email visuals, website banners, lead magnets, PDF guides, and more.

Here's what I create:

  • Launch visuals and countdowns
  • Video snippets and reels (edited from your phone or my drone, if local)
  • Visual storytelling content for your service
  • Consistent branded templates for posts
  • Educational content that builds credibility
  • Thought-leadership visuals

How it fits into the system:

This content fuels your social media, website, email marketing, and even your ads. Everything looks and feels cohesive - giving your brand a premium and trustworthy appearance across all platforms.

3

Web Design

Your brand's digital home should convert - not confuse

If your website isn't bringing you leads, booking calls, or converting visitors into paying clients it's not doing its job. I build clean, strategy-backed websites and funnels that do more than look nice - they drive results.

What I provide:

  • Website wireframe design and content strategy
  • Home, About, Services, Contact, FAQ, and optional Blog pages
  • One-page sales funnels or multi-page websites
  • Mobile-optimized, fast-loading design
  • Booking integrations (Calendly, Acuity, Google Calendar)
  • Lead magnet opt-ins and email capture
  • Easy-to-update backend (you own it fully)

How it supports the rest:

All your marketing leads to your website. Your ads drive traffic here. Your social bio links here. Your email lead magnet lives here. Without it, everything else falls flat. It's your conversion hub.

4

Search Engine Optimization

Be found on Google without paying for every click

SEO is what helps people find your business organically when they search for your services online. Whether you're a virtual coach or a physical clinic, showing up in search results builds trust and visibility without extra ad spend.

SEO elements I manage:

  • Keyword research specific to your location or niche
  • On-page SEO: titles, meta descriptions, URLs, image tags
  • Internal linking for better site structure
  • Page performance optimization
  • Local SEO optimization (Google Business, etc.)
  • Content suggestions for long-term growth

How it ties in:

SEO makes sure your marketing isn't just working today - it keeps working long after the post or campaign is done. It supports your web traffic, strengthens your content, and reduces your long-term dependency on ads.

5

Email Marketing

Don't just rely on likes - build a list you own

Social media can disappear overnight, but email stays yours. This is how you stay connected to potential clients, nurture them over time, and sell without shouting.

Here's what I set up:

  • Email platform setup (ConvertKit, MailerLite, Mailchimp, etc.)
  • Branded welcome sequences
  • Monthly newsletters or weekly updates
  • Campaign emails for product/service launches
  • Automated sequences for onboarding, reminders, etc.
  • Lead magnet integration (PDF guides, checklists, mini trainings)

How it fits:

When someone sees your Instagram post → visits your site → downloads your freebie → they now enter your email funnel where they can receive consistent, helpful, and persuasive content until they're ready to buy.

6

Paid Ads

Let's turn the faucet on and attract more dream clients faster

When you're ready to grow faster, paid ads help you scale your reach beyond your followers. I'll help you run strategic ads that convert - not just boost random posts.

What's included:

  • Ad strategy session based on your offer
  • Ad creative (visuals and copy)
  • Target audience setup, pixel installation
  • Campaign launch + A/B testing
  • Weekly performance monitoring and optimization
  • Funnel integration (send traffic to lead magnet, offer, booking, etc.)

How it works in sync:

Ads send traffic to your landing page or lead magnet → your website captures it → your email nurtures it → your content builds credibility → your strategy turns that traffic into paid clients. It's a full journey - not just a one-off sale.
